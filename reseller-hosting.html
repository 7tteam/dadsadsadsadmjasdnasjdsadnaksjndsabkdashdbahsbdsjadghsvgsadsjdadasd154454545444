What is Reseller Hosting?

Reseller hosting is a form of web hosting wherein the account owner has the ability to use their allotted hard drive space and bandwidth to host websites on behalf of third parties. The reseller purchases the host's services wholesale and then sells them to customers, possibly for a profit.

With Nutro Cloud's reseller hosting plans, you get all the tools and resources needed to start your own web hosting business. Our white-label solutions allow you to brand the hosting services as your own, giving you complete control over pricing, packages, and customer relationships.

Why Choose Reseller Hosting?

Reseller hosting is perfect for web designers, developers, and entrepreneurs who want to offer hosting services to their clients without the technical complexity and high costs of managing servers. It's an excellent way to generate recurring revenue while providing value to your customers.

How Does Reseller Hosting Work?

When you sign up for our reseller hosting plan, you receive a master hosting account with allocated disk space, bandwidth, and the ability to create individual hosting accounts for your customers. You can set your own prices, create custom hosting packages, and manage all aspects of your hosting business through our intuitive control panels.

The process is straightforward: you purchase hosting resources from us at wholesale prices, then resell them to your customers at retail prices. The difference between what you pay us and what you charge your customers is your profit. It's that simple!
