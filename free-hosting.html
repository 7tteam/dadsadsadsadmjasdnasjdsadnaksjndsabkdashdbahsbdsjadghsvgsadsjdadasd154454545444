Frequently asked questions

What is free hosting?

Free hosting is a service that allows users to host their websites without any cost. It comes with basic resources and features suitable for small projects, personal websites, or testing purposes.

What are the limitations of free hosting?

Free hosting plans usually come with limitations on storage, bandwidth, CPU usage, and support. They may also include ads or restrictions on certain features available in premium plans.

Can I upgrade to a premium plan later?

Yes! If you need more resources, better performance, or additional features, you can easily upgrade to one of our premium hosting plans at any time.

Do you provide a free domain name?

Most free hosting plans do not include a free domain name. However, you can use a subdomain (e.g., yoursite.nutro.cloud) or purchase a custom domain separately.
